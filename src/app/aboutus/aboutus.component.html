Our Story

Caring for Your Loved Ones Since 2013

Luton Friendship Homecarers was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide the highest quality home care services that enable people to live independently in the comfort of their own homes.

What started as a small team of dedicated carers has grown into one of Luton's most trusted home care providers. Our success is built on the relationships we form with our clients and their families, and the exceptional care our trained professionals deliver every day.

We understand that choosing a care provider is one of the most important decisions you'll make. That's why we're committed to transparency, excellence, and putting your loved ones first in everything we do.
